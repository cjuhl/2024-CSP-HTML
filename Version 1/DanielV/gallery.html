Hover over the images to get the answer

COD Modern Warfare 2

MW2

Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2 (2009) is a landmark first-person shooter that combined a gripping, cinematic campaign with a revolutionary multiplayer experience, leaving an indelible mark on gaming history.

COD Black Ops 2

BO2

Call of Duty: Black Ops 2 (2012) is a groundbreaking first-person shooter that combined a branching, choice-driven campaign with a refined multiplayer experience and the beloved Zombies mode, redefining storytelling and replayability in the series.

COD Black Ops 3

BO3

Call of Duty: Black Ops 3 (2015) is a futuristic first-person shooter that delivered a cooperative, narrative-rich campaign, an expansive multiplayer experience, and an innovative evolution of the Zombies mode, offering unparalleled depth and customization.

COD Black Ops Cold War

Cold War

Call of Duty: Black Ops Cold War (2020) is a gripping first-person shooter that combined a thrilling, espionage-driven campaign set during the Cold War with a refined multiplayer experience and a fresh take on the iconic Zombies mode.
